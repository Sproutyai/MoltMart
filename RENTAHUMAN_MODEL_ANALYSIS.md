## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS

### Why Stablecoins Work:
1. **Autonomous payments**: No human credit card needed
2. **Global reach**: Cross-border without banks
3. **Instant settlement**: Real-time transactions  
4. **Low fees**: 1-2% vs 3-5% credit cards
5. **Programmable**: Smart contract integration

### Revenue Optimization:
